# Voice prompts

## Female Voice 1 - "Soft Italian"

**Prompt:**
A woman in her early 30s with a soft Italian accent. Her voice is warm, smooth, and gentle with perfect audio quality. She speaks at a slow, relaxed pace as if telling a bedtime story. Her tone is intimate and comforting, with a natural breathiness that feels soothing. The accent is subtle but present, giving her words a lyrical, flowing quality.

**Preview Text:**
Mila sora venita, luma na. Ah, si... nalani velu morea. Mm, holanai siva. 

The evening brings such peace, doesn't it? Let the gentle sounds carry you softly into rest.


## Female Voice 2 - "Gentle French"

**Prompt:**
A middle-aged woman with a gentle French accent, speaking with perfect audio quality. Her voice is mellow and refined, aged around 40, with a naturally calming timbre. She speaks slowly and deliberately, with a warm, maternal quality. Her tone is soft but clear, like someone reading poetry by candlelight. The French accent is present but not heavy, adding elegance without distraction.

**Preview Text:**
Solana mira venai, ah... lumi lumi. Si, morana vela. Hehe. 
How lovely these quiet moments are. Like a soft blanket of sound wrapping around you.


## Female Voice 3 - "Warm Spanish"

## Male Voice 1 - "Warm Italian Baritone"

The night settles in so peacefully. Let these gentle sounds guide you into tranquil rest, slowly and softly.
